Business model

How to structure, position, and grow your analytics freelance consulting business — from zero clients to stable retainer income.

What is this business?

🎯

Analytics & MarTech Consultant

You are a B2B specialist who helps companies properly track their digital products. Your clients are companies — typically in fintech, insurance, e-commerce, and banking — who have analytics needs but no in-house specialist. Your edge: deep enterprise Adobe Analytics + GA4 experience AND hands-on Ionic/Angular mobile implementation — a combination that commands premium rates.

How to find clients

💼

LinkedIn (highest priority)

  • Headline: "Adobe Analytics & GA4 Specialist | Ionic Apps | MarTech"
  • Post weekly: tips, screenshots, case studies from your Sun Life/HSBC experience (anonymized)
  • Connect with: Digital Marketing Managers, CMOs, Product Managers, Heads of Digital
  • DM them: "I noticed your app doesn't have proper event tracking — I can fix that in 2 weeks."
🌐

Upwork / Toptal / Contra

  • Keywords: "Adobe Analytics implementation," "GTM," "Tealium iQ," "mobile analytics," "Ionic"
  • Start with 2–3 competitive bids to build reviews, then raise rates
  • Complete your profile 100% — Upwork penalizes incomplete profiles
✉️

Cold email outreach

  • Target mid-size fintech, insurance, and e-commerce companies
  • Use Hunter.io or Apollo.io to find decision-maker emails
  • Formula: [Problem] → [How you solve it] → ["15-minute call?"]
  • Send 20–30 per week. Expect 5–10% response rate.
🤝

Referrals and agency partners

  • Tell every former colleague what you now offer
  • Partner with web design agencies and dev shops — they need analytics people
  • Offer a 10% finder's fee for any referred paying client

Your pitch

"I help companies implement and manage digital analytics so they can understand how users behave on their websites and apps, reduce wasted ad spend, and make better product decisions. I specialize in Adobe Analytics, Google Analytics 4, and tag management via Tealium iQ, Adobe Launch, and GTM — including hybrid apps built with Ionic/Angular and native iOS/Android apps."

Delivery model

📦

Project-based (start here)

  • Fixed scope, fixed price, fixed timeline
  • Best for implementations, migrations, and setup tasks
  • 50% upfront, 50% on delivery — always
🔁

Retainer (target after 3 clients)

  • Monthly fee for SEO, analytics monitoring, accessibility, dashboards
  • ₱15,000–₱40,000/month per client depending on scope
  • Aim for 2–3 retainer clients alongside project work

How to quote a client

🎙️

Free discovery call first

Always do a free 30-minute discovery call before quoting. Ask: What platform are you on? What do you need to track? What's the deadline? Do you have a dev team?

📊

Written proposal within 24 hrs

Break the price into phases: Discovery & Specs (15%), Implementation (65%), QA & Handover (20%). Add a clear timeline. Use the Contracts tab for the legal portion.

💡

Pricing rules

  • Always collect 50% upfront before starting
  • Add 20–30% buffer to estimated hours
  • Rush jobs (<5 business days): +25–50% premium
  • International clients: quote in USD always
